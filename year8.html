Mathematics

Mathematic teachers in this tutorial centre are devoted in teaching the Cambridge International Mathematics (IGCSE). This curriculum and qualification are implemented to prepare students to utilize the power of mathematics in an increasingly technological world. The mathematics syllabus here entirely fits the syllabus in an international school, thus leads to a qualification with popular tertiary education recognition. The Year 7 Math program are implemented to allow students to enforce the fundamental of mathematical skills relevant to further study. We provide advanced materials that are specially curated to give student a competitive edge in all streams. Our teachers cater to each student’s needs through personal coaching and tutorials on school topics while the pace and flow of each class is dependent on the abilities of students. This is indicated by different level of difficulty of mathematical questions in each chapter of the curriculum. We also provide a conducive learning environment accompanied with multi-resources to instill our students’ interest in Math. They are trained to be competent in interpreting mathematical results and understand their significance. Besides, students are able to appreciate the global aspect of mathematics, its cultural and historical significance and its role in the actual world. Efforts are taken as well in preparing assessment for the students to assess their level of understanding in mathematics. We are able to test their ability to understand and apply concepts from all the aspects of mathematics. On the other hand, their capability on applying mathematical skills and techniques to solve a problem are assessed. From here, they also get to know their capability in drawing logical conclusions from information and master the significance of mathematical or statistical results.
