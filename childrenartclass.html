Children Art Class

Art is a powerful tool that helps kids to process their experiences and deal with their emotions. Art classes also provide beneficial sensory input that can galvanize their imagination and creativity.

Our Children Art Class is suitable for children aged 4 tot 12 years old. It is an opportunity to discover the pleasure of creating art amongst peers. It allows self-expression integral to personal development, observation enhancement and confidence building.

What will the child learn?

Draw, paint and construct based upon their world from their observation, feeling and imagination.

Draw, paint and create object, sculptures of places, people and inventions in the world of their dreams.

Draw, paint, construct and collage landscape, still life and portraiture with a variety of materials based on their observations and imagination.

Expand the students' approach to creative expression by creating both 2-dimensional and 3-dimensional works using different materials and formats.
