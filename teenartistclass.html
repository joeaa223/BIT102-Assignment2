Teen Artist Class

Day & Time:

Saturday | 2:00 to 3:30 pm | 3:30 to 5:00 pm

Sunday | 1:00 to 2:30 pm | 2:30 to 4:00 pm

Venue:

1. 38-1 & 38-2 Jalan Tanjung SD 13/1, Bandar Sri Damansara, 52200 Kuala Lumpur.

2. 48C, Jln Mamanda 9, Tmn Dato Ahmad Razali, 68000, Ampang, Selangor.

Inspire your teens' creative side with our specially designed art lessons.Our classes will help unleash their passion and encourage creative expression while building on their artistic skills.

This class is designed to develop the eye, hand, and mind of a young artist-beginner or advanced. With drawing as the foundation, students learn to observe, interpret and design. Through a series of exercises, they gather a variety of skills and techniques to build a vocabulary with which they will use to create their own artwork. Projects incorporate a broad range of approaches from tradisional subjects, working with variety mediums. Through this class, learners will learn and develop the skills of research, information and studies of an artist.
