Have Fun Tuition is an education centre that focuses on helping A Level students achieve their highest potential through very effective tuition classes and score in examinations.



About A-Level

A-Level is a Malaysian pre-university program modeled after the UK education system. Similar to STPM (Malaysian Higher School Certificate), A-Level prepares students for university degrees by allowing them to study a variety of subjects in depth.



A-Levels Requirements in Malaysia

A-levels in Malaysia, like other pre-university courses, require a minimum of 5 Cs in SPM, O-Level, or equivalent. There are other pre-university programmes, such as Australian Matriculation, Canadian Pre-University (CPU), and STPM, that are equal to A-levels.

Subject prerequisites are required for some A-Level topics, such as:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Further Mathematics

Developing Comprehensive Abilities

A-Level courses foster critical thinking, independent learning, and problem-solving skills. Students choose subjects based on their interests and goals, including humanities, sciences, and business. The curriculum offers broad and in-depth knowledge, equipping students with comprehensive skills.

In-Depth Subject Knowledge

A-Level emphasizes providing in-depth subject knowledge. Students deepen their understanding of the chosen subjects by studying specific topics and concepts, preparing them for further academic research and career development.

Excellent Grades and University Admissions

A-Level studies lead to excellent grades and university admissions. The A-Level certificate is widely recognized by prestigious international institutions.

Our A-Level program aims to provide students with a comprehensive, in-depth, and challenging academic education. Through high-level subject knowledge and the development of comprehensive abilities, we help students build a strong foundation for future academic and career pursuits.
