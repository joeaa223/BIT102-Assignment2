Chemistry

Our Chemistry lessons build a firm foundation in essential content and process skills for our students to achieve academic excellence in the subject. We aim to allow our students to acquire sufficient knowledge and understanding to be suitably prepared for studies beyond Cambridge IGCSE. Students will be taught the topic through notes, tutorials and concept maps. Our materials facilitate the understanding and internalisation of content, and are dedicated to introducing and practising essential exam skills.

Additional Mathematics

Mathematics

Mandarin Language

Through our Mandarin classes, our students can obtain insights into the culture and society of countries where the language is spoken while being a fluent user of the language. Accounting

Our Accounting lessons allow students to develop knowledge and understanding of the principles and purposes of accounting for individuals, businesses, non-trading organisations and society as a whole. Our students have an understanding of accounting principles, policies, techniques, procedures and terminology as well as improved skills of numeracy, literacy, communication, enquiry, presentation and interpretation.

Business Studies

Our lessons ensure students apply their knowledge and critical understanding to current issues and problems in a wide range of business contexts. We aim to help students develop knowledge and understanding of the major groups and organisations within and outside business, and consider ways in which they are able to influence objectives, decisions and activities.

Economics

Economics is a subject that develops students’ ability to use the tools of economic analysis in particular situations. Our lessons can develop their ability to use economic skills to understand better the world in which they live and to further their understanding of the economies of developed and developing nations and of the relationships between them.

Geography

The aim of our Geography lessons is to encourage students to develop an understanding of location on a local, regional and global scale. We also seek to promote an awareness of the characteristics, distribution and processes affecting contrasting physical and human environments. Through the lessons, our students can understand the ways in which people interact with each other and with their environment.

History

We seek to stimulate an interest in and enthusiasm for learning about the past for students. Our lessons promote the acquisition of knowledge and understanding of individuals, people and societies in the past and ensure that learners’ knowledge is rooted in an understanding of the nature and use of historical evidence. Students are also encouraged to develop of historical skills, including investigation, analysis and communication skills.

Information & Communication Technology (ICT)

Our IGCSE Information and Communication Technology lessons aim to develop knowledge of ICT including new and emerging technologies and skills to enhance work produced in a range of contexts. Students are able to obtain skills to analyse, design, implement, test and evaluate ICT systems as well as skills to consider the impact of current and new technologies.
